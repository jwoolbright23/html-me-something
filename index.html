States That We Have Visited

  • Breckenridge, Colorado
  • Norfolk, Virginia
  • Manhattan, New York

Breckenridge, Colorado

Breckenridge, Colorado was the first vacation that the two of us went on together. On our way to Breckenridge we stopped at Mount Evans and hiked our way to the top. The peak is an astonishing 14,271 feet high making it the highest summit of the Chicago Peaks.

Norfolk, Virginia

Norfolk, Virginia was the second trip we went on in 2017. We stayed in Ghent, spent a lot of time at Virginia Beach, and also visited my girlfriend's graduate school, Eastern Virginia Medical School. We also took a tour of the Norfolk Naval Base which is the largest Naval base in the world.

Manhattan, New York

Manhattan, New York was the third trip we took in 2017. We stayed in downtown Manhattan at the Intercontinental on Lexington Avenue. On our first night we went to the top of the Empire State Building, walked through Time's Square, and visited Rockefeller Plaza. In addition to that we went to Little Italy, the Brooklyn Bridge, the 9/11 Memorial, and Central Park.
